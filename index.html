Wenqi Wang (王文麒)

I received my Ph.D. degree from School of Industrial Engineering at Purdue University in 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Vaneet Aggarwal. Before that I obtained my B.S. from Department of Physics at Fudan University in 2013, supervised by Prof. Yuanbo Zhang. In the fall of 2012, I was an exchanging student at University of California, Berkeley. My research is about multi-dimensional data and its applications in machine learning and deep learning.
